The service I've chosen to inquire is MyFitnessPal.

Studies have shown that a success of dieting is boiled down to maintaining a consistent caloric deficit. Thus, the calorie counting is an effective strategy to ensure that the dieting effort is successful. With mobile devices, it is easier than ever to obtain and log such information. However, given how eating habits can be frequent and unpredictable, the experience of logging every food item must flow smoothly. For this assignment, I examine how the user logs their food items, identify key aspects of the process and prototype a solution.
Next: Contextual Inquiry

Contextual Inquiry

To identify the problem, I had an experienced user show me how they use the app to submit their food entry.

Meal

Before the user can submit a food item, he must first pick out which meal the food item was eaten as. There was a brief moment of pause, as the user was scanning past the extraneous information for the button that would allow him to submit food.

Food Item

The user searches for the food item in the search bar. There were few factors that made his search easier. He had a preference for items with green check marks as it meant the items nutrition fact was accurate.

Serving Size

User has to pick out a serving size.

Confirmation

User scans through the nutrition information before pressing check to add the food.
